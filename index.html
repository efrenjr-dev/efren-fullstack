Checkpoint - ECommerce (MERN Stack)

Developed an e-commerce web-app built with MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js and Node.js) as a capstone project at the Zuitt Coding Bootcamp. This is a completely functional web-app where one can visit, register, purchase and make a transaction with demo products.

Imus Agricultural Portal

An Agricultural portal developed in PHP and SQL for the benefit of the agricultural sector of Imus, Cavite including farmers, agricultural technologists, market co-operatives, and traders. Manages the city’s distribution of free crop seeds, fertilizer, and livestock medicine. Informs live gate prices for selected crops. Features SMS capabilities to reach technology-challenged farmers. The project received the Outstanding Contribution to the Department Award and Social Relevance Award from the College of Computer Studies at De La Salle University - Manila.
